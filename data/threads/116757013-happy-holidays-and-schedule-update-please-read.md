# Happy holidays and schedule update - please read

## Message 1 (#116) — Mark Novak — 2025-12-13 01:32 UTC

Happy holidays to all! Take a moment to recognize all the work we've come together to accomplish. We've published two IETF drafts this year, have a decent first draft of the Reference Architecture, and have started some movement in the industry (just look at
the Trustee presentation from this week's TAC!). All in all, a great year, and we haven't even had our first anniversary yet!

We are not having any more TWI meetings for the rest of this calendar year. We are, however, going to have a very interesting first meeting of 2026 on January 6. In that meeting, we are hoping to start a conversation about bringing WIMSE and RATS closer together,
and decide what we choose to submit to IETF 125.

As such, I am not going to send a separate agenda the day before as I usually do, so mark your calendars now.

See you soon and enjoy the break!
