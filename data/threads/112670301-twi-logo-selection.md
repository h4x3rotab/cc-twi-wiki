## Message 2 (#18) — Mark Novak — 2025-05-08 14:31 UTC

Personally I'm not a big fan of either of the options listed.

The first (top) one is the closest because it has the identity theme, but on the left is shows a person where it should be showing something machine/workload/robot related.

The middle one is too generic.

The last one has the biometric theme, but again, machines don't have human fingerprints.

I'd like to request that they perhaps look into changing the top option to replace the human icon with a robot one.
