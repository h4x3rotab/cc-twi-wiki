## Message 2 (#40) — Mark Novak — 2025-05-27 16:30 UTC

Here's my proposed first draft:

The TWI SIG seeks to add trustworthiness properties to the ongoing industry efforts around workload identity. Confidential Computing's data-in-use protections make it possible for the first time to strongly bind identities to workload instances and to utilize
remote attestation to imbue credentials with provenance. Giving workloads trustworthy identities will ease the job of uplifting existing applications to utilize Confidential Computing, further helping its universal adoption. The TWI SIG charter can be found
[here](https://github.com/confidential-computing/governance/blob/main/SIGs/TWI/TWI_Charter.md).
