# Lesson from SessionShark

## Message 1 (#12) — Mark Novak — 2025-04-28 13:52 UTC

The SessionShark phishing kit has just been released.

 

<https://slashnext.com/blog/sessionshark-steals-session-tokens-to-slip-past-office-365-mfa/>

 

Another stark reminder that turning multi-factor authentication into a bearer token negates the benefits of the former while giving the adopter of MFA a false sense of security. This is why in TWI I hope to
fight tooth and nail against treating bearer workload identity tokens as trustworthy.
