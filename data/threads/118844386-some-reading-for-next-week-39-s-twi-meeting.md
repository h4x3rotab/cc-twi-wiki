# Some reading for next week&#39;s TWI meeting

## Message 1 (#152) — Mark Novak — 2026-04-15 17:00 UTC

The following documents have caught my attention and may be worth a review in one of the near-term TWI SIG meetings. Listed in the decreasing order of priority.

1. Section 3.1.1 (bootstrapping) of the latest
   [WIMSE Reference Architecture](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/draft-ietf-wimse-arch/) draft
2. [Carrying Remote Attestation
   Evidence in Workload Identity Tokens](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/draft-liu-wimse-wit-attestation/)
3. [AI Agent Authentication and Authorization](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/draft-klrc-aiagent-auth/) (from Network Working Group,
   not WIMSE)
4. [Transitive
   Attestation for Sovereign Workloads: A WIMSE Profile](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/draft-mw-wimse-transitive-attestation/)
5. [WIMSE Applicability
   for AI Agents](https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/draft-ni-wimse-ai-agent-identity/)
