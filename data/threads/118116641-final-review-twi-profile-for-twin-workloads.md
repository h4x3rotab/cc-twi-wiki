# Final review: TWI Profile for Twin Workloads

## Message 1 (#132) — Mark Novak — 2026-03-03 16:40 UTC

In today's meeting we have largely completed the work on defining credential provisioning and credential acquisition for "twin workloads." The term "twin workloads" is new — it simply means workloads that share code, security-sensitive configuration, and other
compliance-relevant attributes such as location — but the document itself is mostly unchanged.

Review the latest version here before we go to the next step of socializing it with WIMSE, RATS and the Attestation SIG.
