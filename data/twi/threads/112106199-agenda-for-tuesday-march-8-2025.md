# Agenda for Tuesday March 8, 2025

## Message 1 (#3) — Mark Novak — 2025-04-05 19:40 UTC

Hello all,

This upcoming Tuesday, I propose we take up the following topics, in some order:

* Make a plan for the SIG's contributions to the Madrid IETF
* Review the portion of the Reference Architecture that I have authored related to making both long-lived and short-lived workloads obtain their credentials in a manner best suited to the lifetime of the workload
* Make a list of next most important tasks for the SIG to handle, try and prioritize it, decide on how issues will be tracked

As always, anyone can suggest additional topics for discussion.

See you soon.
