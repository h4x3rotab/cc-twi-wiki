# Agenda for Tuesday October 7, 2025

## Message 1 (#96) — Mark Novak — 2025-10-06 22:26 UTC

Tomorrow we'll continue work on our Informational Draft for IETF 124, now called TWIX.

I have added a lot of content to the draft, please review before the meeting if possible. Here's the link:
<https://docs.google.com/document/d/1O29GZyRTHaMss6x23eBGnkUMhTEg72JvV0oCf8Ug7wQ/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.7hnowgobwq7z>

Things to pay attention to in particular when reviewing:

1. There is an extensive motivation section. May be a bit too long for an I-D, but it explains why it is we need this, and I have found from talking to multiple people that this is not intuitive to them, although
   of course it is to us, being immersed in it.
2. I have outlined five different options for a Workload to obtain its Identity Credentials. Not all of these are currently reflected in the Reference Architecture, but eventually there should be parity between the
   two documents. Also the I-D currently does not yet contain the swimlanes — after we agree on the options, I'll add them. Again, I aim for parity between the two documents so there will not be any duplication of work.
