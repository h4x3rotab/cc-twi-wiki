Hello fellow WIMSE enthusiasts,  
  
Following our packed agenda and extensive conversations in Madrid, there was significant interest in hosting several interims for WIMSE to continue and complete our work, especially with a few documents approaching WGLC readiness. The chairs have worked through
our notes and we’ve identified four topics that we plan to have WIMSE interims for.  
  
The chairs would like to propose that we choose a regular time and cadence for these meetings — for example, if all the meetings were at 2PM GMT on Tuesdays every other week, people could work out schedules around that where possible. Our target timeframe for
these meetings is between the weeks of September 8 and October 20, with the possibility of pulling one meeting into late August if there’s scheduling and interest alignment. If you have opinions on this approach, please raise them here on the list, but we’ll
be putting together a poll soon to figure out a good time slot.  
  
In particular, we are proposing four interims for the working group, as follows:  
  
- Workload to Workload, draft issues discussion and working session  
- Agentic AI use cases for WIMSE  
- WIMSE architecture, draft issues and working session  
- Workload to Workload, preparation for WGLC  
  
We’d particularly like to make sure that the two W2W discussions are at least a month apart to allow for progress in between calls.  
  
The chairs will be reaching out to the authors and proposers of each section separately to confirm availability and targeted content, but watch the list for a request for timing.  
  
Thank you,  
  
 — Justin & Pieter
